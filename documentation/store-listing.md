## Full Description (App Store)

Train With Joe helps you build lasting vocabulary through personalized training sessions. Practice words at your own pace, track your progress, and watch your language skills grow — whether you're picking up a new language or strengthening the one you already speak.

The app adapts to how you learn. Each training session focuses on the words you need to practice most, using difficulty ratings and smart repetition to help you retain what you've learned.

How it works:
- Start a training session from any of your vocabulary lists
- Practice words with translations, definitions, example sentences, and difficulty levels
- Track your progress with detailed training statistics
- Review past sessions and see how your skills improve over time
- Create vocabulary lists from real-world photos — snap a picture and let AI extract relevant words

Building your word lists is easy. Take a photo or pick one from your gallery, choose your source and target languages (or let the app auto-detect them), and the AI builds a vocabulary list ready for training.

Supported languages include English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese.

Features:
- Personalized training sessions with progress tracking
- Training statistics: daily, per-session, and overview
- Difficulty-based word practice
- AI-powered vocabulary extraction from photos
- Automatic language detection
- Word details: translation, definition, part of speech, example sentence, difficulty
- Vocabulary list management (save, rename, delete, export)
- Export lists as text for use in other apps
- Secure sign-in with email verification
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app

A subscription unlocks full access to all features.

## Review Notes (App Store)

To review the app, please follow these steps:

1. Open the app and sign up with any email address. You will receive a verification code via email — enter it to complete sign-up.
2. Once signed in, you can create a vocabulary list by tapping the "+" button. Pick a photo from your gallery or take a new one, select your languages, and the app will generate a vocabulary list from the image.
3. From your vocabulary lists, tap on a list to view the words. You can rename, delete, or export the list.
4. To start training, go to the "Training" tab. Create a new training session by selecting one or more vocabulary lists, choosing a training mode (text input, multiple choice, or AI training), and starting the session.
5. During training, answer the prompts for each word. After completing a session, you can view your results and track your progress over time in the training statistics.
6. The app offers a free tier with limited image scans and vocabulary lists. A subscription (Basic or Pro) unlocks higher limits and AI training mode. You can subscribe via the in-app purchase flow.
7. External links (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Contact Us) and in-app purchases are gated behind a parental gate that requires solving a simple math problem. This ensures children cannot access external content or make purchases without parental permission.
8. To delete your account, go to the Info tab and tap "Delete Account". A confirmation dialog will appear. After confirming, all user data (vocabulary lists, trainings, usage counters, and subscription records) will be permanently deleted.

No demo account is needed — any new sign-up will work. The app requires an internet connection for AI features and account sync.

Privacy & Data Collection:
- The app does not include any third-party analytics SDKs.
- The app does not include any third-party advertising.
- No user data is shared with third parties.
- The app collects only the data necessary for its core functionality: email for authentication (via AWS Cognito), vocabulary lists, training progress, and subscription status. All data is stored securely in AWS (DynamoDB, S3) and is not used for any purpose beyond providing the app's features.
