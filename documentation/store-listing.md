## Full Description (App Store & Play Store)

Train With Joe turns everyday images into vocabulary lessons. Take a photo or pick one from your gallery — the app uses AI to identify words in the image and builds a vocabulary list with translations, definitions, example sentences, and difficulty levels.

Whether you're learning a new language or helping your kids build their word skills, Train With Joe makes it easy and visual.

How it works:
- Snap a photo or pick images from your gallery
- Choose source and target languages, or let the app auto-detect them
- AI analyzes the images and extracts relevant vocabulary
- Each word comes with a translation, definition, part of speech, example sentence, and difficulty rating
- Save, rename, export, and manage your vocabulary lists

Supported languages include English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese.

Features:
- AI-powered image vocabulary analysis
- Multi-image scanning in a single session
- Automatic language detection
- Word details: translation, definition, part of speech, example sentence, difficulty
- Vocabulary list management (save, rename, delete, export)
- Export lists as text for use in other apps
- Works on iOS, Android, and web
- Secure sign-in with email verification

A subscription unlocks full access to all features.
